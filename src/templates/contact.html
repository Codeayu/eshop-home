Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. Free shipping on orders over $50.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package on our website or the carrier's site.
